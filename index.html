What is Lynx Security?

Lynx Security is a security company devoted to performing white-box and black-box audits in order to accomplish 4 important goals:



Identify

Identify vulnerabilities in web applications and software.

Isolate

Isolate vulnerabilities and determine the root causes.

Assess

Assess and determine the impacts of vulnerabilities

Mitigate

Mitigate vulnerabilities by creating and deploying proper patches.
